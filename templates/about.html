A lil’ bit about me

Hi there, I’m Dani. I’m a UX/UI designer based in the Bay Area with a background in visual arts and graphic design.

My Vietnamese heritage and journeys in the US have led me to connect with people from different countries and taught me to consider a design from a cultural perspective.

During my earlier education, studying STEM and competing in olympiads have shaped my analytical skills and love for a good challenge.

On the other hand, I have been drawing since I learned to pick up a pencil. As I explored different art and design areas, I have developed an eye for visual defsign and a detail-oriented mindset.

In the end, I realized that I wanted to make something that could be used by others instead of just being on display. UI/UX just clicks! My experiences living in a multicultural environment and working across multiple disciplines now serve as valuable pillars to strengthen my UX design work.
